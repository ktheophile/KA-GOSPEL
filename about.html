    • ABOUT US:

    "KA-GOSPEL" is a Music platform based on Christian Back ground and philosophy,
    established in 2024 with a desire to lift and enable Rwandan music and musicians
    to make their dreams true and to compete on the international scene. With a
    mission to play a key role in strengthening the Music industry of Rwanda, by
    researching, training, promoting, producing, preserving and developing the country's
    musical heritage; our music teaching and producing program caters for motivated
    students of all ages and backgrounds, from beginners to professional levels, and we
    always give maximum opportunity to develop talents at affordable rates.

    GOALS & OBJECTIVES

    •To build a great foundation to children through many benefits that music provides,
    such as: boosting their brain power, improving their memory, helping them socially,
    building their confidence, patience, discipline...,fostering their creativity, etc.

    •To give skills young people so that they can make living from Art & music ( Singers, Instrumentalist, Producers, Artist Managers, Vocal Trainers, Song writers, Composers, Music Teachers, Creative Artists...)

    •To avail opportunities for future artists/musicians to catch up with international music challenges in being able to play at least an instruments and/or do live vocal performance

    •To establish a music library service that will provide all necessary means for students pursuing their musical careers, preserving and developing the musical heritage of Rwanda locally, regional and internationally.

    •To promote professional ex cellence among musicians through training, seminars, workshops, debates...
